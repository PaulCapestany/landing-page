What Problem Does bitiq Solve?

Today's digital world lacks effective accountability mechanisms, enabling misinformation and low-quality content to thrive unchecked. We've unknowingly surrendered control over our digital experiences to algorithms designed to maximize clicks and engagement, not truth or quality. bitiq fixes this.

How bitiq Works

Unlike typical tech platforms driven by opaque algorithms that prioritize engagement and profit, bitiq empowers users by establishing relevance rankings through a Web-of-Trust consisting of secure open-protocol identities, along with auditable algorithms and AI.
